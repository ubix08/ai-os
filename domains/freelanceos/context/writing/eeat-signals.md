# E-E-A-T Signals Guide

## Experience Signals
- First-hand examples from actual projects
- Screenshots of real terminal output, error messages, dashboards
- Before/after comparisons showing real results
- Personal anecdotes about challenges faced and solved

## Expertise Signals
- Author bio with relevant credentials and experience
- Links to prior published work or open-source contributions
- Technical depth: dive into implementation details
- Accurate version numbers and dependency specifications
- Discussion of trade-offs and alternatives (shows real understanding)

## Authority Signals
- At least 3 sourced statistics from recognized sources
- Links to official documentation and specification
- References to recognized tools, frameworks, and platforms
- Benchmarks and performance comparisons
- Citations from industry leaders and reputable publications

## Trust Signals
- Publication date and last-updated date in frontmatter
- Clear methodology — explain how conclusions were reached
- Transparent about affiliations and disclosures
- Changelog for updated articles
- No affiliate links without disclosure

## Quick Checklist
- [ ] First-hand example in at least one section
- [ ] Author bio with real credentials
- [ ] 3+ sourced statistics with links
- [ ] Publication and update dates visible
- [ ] Trade-offs discussed for technical decisions
- [ ] No fabricated claims or statistics
- [ ] Official documentation linked where applicable
- [ ] Screenshot or terminal output for key demonstrations
